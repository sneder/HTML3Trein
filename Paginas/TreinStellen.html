The Enlengthened Inter-Regio Material is a train type that the NS uses as an intercity. VIRM-6 and VIRM-4 are the only ones still currently in use. VIRM-6 is a train with 6 carriages, whereas VIRM-4 is a train with 4 carriages. They can be connected together interchangibaly. For example a VIRM-6 with a VIRM-4 to make a 10 carriage train. Also 3 VIRM-4's or 2 VIRM-6's to make a 12 carriage train. Etc.
